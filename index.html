A century in one club

You run a sporting institution for 100 years, and the world keeps changing the rules underneath you.

You don't pick a starting eleven. You run the institution that hires whoever does. A handful of heavy decisions across a century, and you live with each one for the rest of the game.

A choice in 1925 still bites in 1995. That delay is the whole pleasure. Money, soul, fans. None can hit zero. They fight each other.
